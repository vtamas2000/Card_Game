    Game of Life

    Okay! Here comes the Game of Life! If you're not familiar with it, then you should check out the Wikipedia page here.

    You can edit the initial state with the mouse (press or drag) or press "R" for a random starting state. You can play and pause the game with space, and restart any time with "X". To evolve the system only once, press enter.

    You can set the size of the cells below (min. 10px, max. 60px). Note: lower cell size means more cells and decreased performance.


    Langton's Ant

    Aaaand this is the famous Langton's Ant! Believe me it's super cool! What? You haven't heard about it? Click here really fast to go to the Wikipedia page!

    Below you can tell the rule to the ant. With the default settings the ant turns right at one color and left at the other. But check this out! You can set your own rules. Type as many "r" (for right) and "l" (for left) letters as you wish, and the ant will follow these rules. Please don't write spaces or any other characters, or it simply won't work.
